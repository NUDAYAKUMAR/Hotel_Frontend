📖 Our Journey

Started with Passion & Purpose

SVFF(Shree Veerabhadreswara Fast Food) Tiffin Hotel was founded in 2014 by a family that had one simple dream — to serve authentic, home-style South Indian tiffin to the neighbourhood at an honest price.

What started as a tiny roadside stall with just 3 items on the menu has grown into a beloved local institution. Today, we serve over 100 customers every Day, yet our preparation remains as hands-on and personal as day one.

Every idli, every Vada, every serving of pongal is prepared using the same traditional methods our grandmother used — grinding fresh batter overnight, fresh coconut chutney every day and real Taste on every plate.
